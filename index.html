Current Projects

KarmaDrip

KarmaDrip is a decentralized water utility that aims to lower economic barriers to clean water access. Through its unique approach, KarmaDrip gives ownership to consumers, resulting in a more resilient infrastructure.

European Dynamism Podcast

The European Dynamism Podcast explores the challenges Europe is facing economically and how we can overcome them as proposed by the eu/acc movement. Each episode taps into the voices of a network of insiders, entrepreneurs working to turn the tide. Listeners can expect deep dives into innovation, policy, and the broader implications of these efforts, offering valuable perspectives on how to drive positive change in the European arena.

Project Vitae

Project Vitae is my lifelong mission to uncover lost knowledge from ancient human civilizations. Currently, I'm building a tool to aggregate all the links to discover new areas deserving archaeological investigation.
